Instructions

Step 1: Check Your Balances

Once you've made your health and dental selections, review the amounts that you have left in your Health Spending Account and/or Lifestyle Spending Account (if applicable); you can do this by logging into your Great West Life and/or League account.



Remember that your remaining balance on December 31st, 2019 will carry over to 2020 and will be available for 1 year.

Step 2: Plan For Your Upcoming Expenses

Think about the expenses that you or your dependents may have in the upcoming calendar year for services not covered under our basic health and dental plan. Some examples to consider:

Health Care Spending Account
Glasses or contact lenses
Orthodontics
Prescription Drugs
Massage Therapy
Psychology
Lifestyle Spending Account
Gym/Fitness Memberships
Daycare Costs
Travel
Pet Care
Art/Music Classes

Using the calculator below, you can estimate the amounts you wish to contribute to your accounts.

  What are Out-of-Pocket Expenses?

Reimbursement Levels: The percentage of eligible expenses above the deductible that is eligible for reimbursement under the plan.

Plan maximums: The annual maximum of coverage eligible under each benefit.

Example: Massage Therapy has a reimbursement level of 80% and an annual maximum of $300.
